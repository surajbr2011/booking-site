# Exotica Agonda - Video Tutorial Scripts 🎥

*Since AI cannot record voiceovers, please follow this script to record the official training materials for your staff using a screen recorder like Loom or OBS.*

## Video 1: Navigating the Dashboard (2 Minutes)
**Visual:** Screen recording of the Admin Dashboard.
**Script:**
"Welcome to the Exotica Agonda Admin Panel. When you first log in, you will land on the Dashboard. Here, you can see live statistics for our resort. At the top, these cards show Total Bookings, Active Rooms, Pending Inquiries, and Revenue. Below that, you'll see a quick list of Recent Bookings. If you ever need to download this data, simply click the 'Export Report' button right here at the top."

## Video 2: Managing Bookings (3 Minutes)
**Visual:** Screen recording of the Bookings Tab.
**Script:**
"Let's look at managing our guests. Click on the 'Bookings' tab. You'll see a complete list of all reservations. To find a specific guest, use the search bar here. You can also filter by 'Confirmed' or 'Pending' statuses. To view a booking's details, click the eye icon. In this modal, you can update the status if the guest cancels or checks in securely."

## Video 3: Room Inventory Management (2 Minutes)
**Visual:** Screen recording of the Rooms Tab.
**Script:**
"Managing our rooms is easy. Go to the 'Rooms' tab. Here are all our cottages. To add a new one, click 'Add New Room', fill in the category and price, and upload a beautiful photo. You can toggle a room's availability directly from this list to ensure we never double-book."
